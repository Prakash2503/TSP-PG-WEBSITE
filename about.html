About TSP PG

Discover our story, values, and the team behind your comfortable living experience

Our Story

Founded in 2015, TSP PG began with a simple vision: to create a home away from home for students and young professionals. What started as a small 10-room facility has now grown into one of the most trusted PG accommodations in the city, housing over 200 residents across three locations.

Our journey has been guided by the feedback and needs of our residents. Every improvement, every new facility, and every policy change has been implemented with their comfort and convenience in mind.

"We don't just provide rooms, we create communities where residents can thrive academically, professionally, and personally."

Today, TSP PG stands as a testament to what can be achieved when care and quality service come together. We continue to evolve, adopting new technologies and amenities to stay at the forefront of student accommodation.
