Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about TSP PG

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Our standard check-in time is 12:00 PM and check-out is 11:00 AM. However, we can accommodate early check-ins or late check-outs based on availability with prior notice.

What documents are required for booking?

You'll need to provide a government-issued ID proof (Aadhar, Passport, or Driver's License), two passport-size photographs, and proof of student or employment status if applicable.

Is the security deposit refundable?

Yes, the security deposit is fully refundable upon vacating the premises, provided there are no damages to the property and all dues are cleared.

Do you provide meal plans?

Yes, we offer flexible meal plans that include breakfast, lunch, and dinner. You can choose any combination or opt out if you prefer to manage your own meals.

Can I have visitors in my room?

Visitors are allowed in common areas between 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. For privacy and security reasons, visitors are not permitted in resident rooms without prior permission.
